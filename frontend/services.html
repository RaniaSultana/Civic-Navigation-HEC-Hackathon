National Identity Card

A secure, biometric-enabled identity document for all eligible citizens. This card serves as your primary proof of identity, citizenship, and residence for all government and private sector transactions.

id_card
checklist

Required Documents

  • check_circle Original Birth Certificate (Digital or Physical)
  • check_circle Proof of Residency (Utility bill or Lease)
  • check_circle Two recent passport-sized photographs
  • check_circle Previous ID (if renewing or replacing)

Biometric Enrollment

All applicants must visit an authorized center for digital fingerprinting and iris scanning. This ensures the highest level of security for your digital identity.
